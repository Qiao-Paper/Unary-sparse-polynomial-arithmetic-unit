Basic functions of the unary sparse polynomial algorithm:

(1) Create a polynomial. 

(2) Display a polynomial. 

(3) Destroy the polynomial. 

(4) Empty the polynomial. 

(5) Copy polynomial. 

(6) Sum of two sparse polynomials. 

(7) Difference calculation of two sparse polynomials. 

(8) Quadrature of two sparse polynomials. 

(9) Find the n-th derivative of the sparse polynomial. 

(10) Calculate the value of sparse polynomial at x=a. 

(11) calculate the definite integral of the sparse polynomial on the interval [a,b]. 

(12) The sparse polynomial to the n-th power.
